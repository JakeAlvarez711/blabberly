Blabberly support

How to reach us

Email us at support@blabberly.com. We read every message and reply as quickly as we can.

What to include in a report

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • The username or email on your Blabberly account
  • What happened and what you expected to happen
  • A link to or screenshot of the content, if you are reporting a post, comment, or user
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Roughly when the problem occurred

Account deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app: open Settings, then Your Data, then Delete Account. This removes your account and its associated data. If you prefer, you can also email us and we will handle it for you.
