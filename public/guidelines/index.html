Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Blabberly is a community built around sharing authentic food experiences in our local neighborhoods. These guidelines help keep our community welcoming, helpful, and trustworthy for everyone.

The golden rule: Share genuinely, treat others with respect, and keep it about the food.

1. Keep It Authentic

  • Share real experiences. Post about places you've actually visited and food you've actually tried.
  • Be honest. Don't post fake reviews, inflated ratings, or misleading content about any restaurant or dish.
  • Disclose relationships. If you have a personal or business relationship with a restaurant, be transparent about it.

2. Be Respectful

  • Treat others kindly. Disagreements about food are fine — personal attacks are not.
  • Respect restaurant staff and owners. Constructive feedback is welcome; harassment is not.
  • No hate speech. Content that promotes discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic will be removed.
  • No bullying or harassment. Targeting other users with repeated unwanted contact, threats, or intimidation is prohibited.

3. Content Standards

  • Keep it food-focused. Blabberly is about food discovery — stay on topic.
  • No explicit or violent content. Keep photos, videos, and comments appropriate for all ages.
  • No spam. Don't post repetitive content, unsolicited promotions, or irrelevant links.
  • Respect copyright. Only post content you created or have permission to share.
  • No illegal content. Don't post anything that violates local, state, or federal laws.

4. Crew Cut Sessions

  • Respect all participants in collaborative video sessions.
  • Don't record others without their knowledge or consent.
  • The session host is responsible for reviewing the final edit before posting.
  • All participants can approve or decline their inclusion in the final video.

5. Privacy

  • Don't share other people's personal information without their consent.
  • Don't post photos or videos of identifiable individuals without their permission.
  • Respect the privacy of restaurant staff and other diners.

6. Reporting and Enforcement

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it through the App. We review all reports and may take the following actions:

  • Content removal: Posts that violate our guidelines will be removed.
  • Warnings: First-time or minor violations may result in a warning.
  • Account suspension: Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary suspension.
  • Account termination: Severe or ongoing violations will result in permanent removal from the platform.

7. Questions?

If you have questions about these guidelines, reach out to us at support@blabberly.com.
