Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Blabberly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Blabberly mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo.

Content You Create: Photos, videos, reviews, and comments you post through the App.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your precise location to show nearby restaurants, detect places, and power geo-based features like hotspot detection.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, content viewed, and engagement metrics to improve our service.

Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features, including restaurant discovery, social features, and content creation tools
  • To deliver push notifications you've opted into (messages, follows, likes, comments, Crew Cut invites)
  • To personalize your feed using our ranking algorithms
  • To detect geographic hotspots and trending content in your area
  • To power gamification features (XP, quests, awards, streaks)
  • To respond to support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your profile, posts, and activity are visible to other users as part of the social features of the App
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication, data storage, and cloud functions; Expo for push notification delivery; Google Places API for restaurant information
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures including authentication requirements, access controls, and encrypted connections to protect your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Notification Preferences: You can control which notifications you receive in the App's settings
  • Location: You can disable location access through your device settings
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@blabberly.com
  • Data Access: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@blabberly.com.
